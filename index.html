Frequently Asked Questions

How long does window tinting last?

Professionally installed films can last 10 years or more depending on the film type, care, and environment. High-quality ceramic films last even longer.

Is ceramic window tint worth the investment?

Yes. Ceramic tint offers superior heat rejection, UV protection, and durability without interfering with visibility or signals. It’s ideal for long-term performance.

Do you also offer paint protection film?

Yes. Paint protection film (PPF) shields your car’s paint from chips, scratches, and road debris, keeping the finish looking new for years.

What benefits does residential window tinting provide?

Home window films reduce heat, block UV rays, increase privacy, and protect furniture from fading. They also help lower energy bills throughout the year.

Can businesses use window film for branding?

Absolutely. Commercial films include branded storefront graphics, office decals, frosted partitions, and wall wraps that add style and promote your company.
